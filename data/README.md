# Data Description

This project uses Reddit data related to generative AI and mental health discussions.

Due to privacy, ethical considerations, and platform policies, raw data are not included in this repository.
This protocol of this study was reviewed by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) and determined to be exempt from further review (#26-0459).
The study used publicly available Reddit data and did not involve direct interaction with human subjects or the collection of identifiable personal information. 
All data were handled in accordance with institutional guidelines to ensure privacy and confidentiality.

The dataset consisted of:
- PostIDs
- Subreddit source (r/ChatGPT, r/therapyGPT, r/mentalhealth, or r/anxiety)
- Title of Post
- Body of Post
- Timestamps (Post UTC), Excluded from final dataset for security reasons

Data were processed and filtered to include only posts relevant to user experiences with AI chatbots in mental health contexts.
